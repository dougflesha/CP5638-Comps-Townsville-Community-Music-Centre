Goals

The Townsville Community Music Centre (TCMC) website is intended to promote concerts and events organized by Townsville Community Music Centre (TCMC) to increase the sale of tickets, the number of new members and engaged the active participation of members, artists and the public.

Success Evaluation

The trial period to measure the effectiveness of the website will be six months. The website will have a link to register new members. After six months, the number of registrations made using the TCMC website will be greater than the number of registrations made at the TCMC office. There is only one TCMC office and no other branch is planned.

Target Audience

Artists and audience with specialised tastes from North Queensland
